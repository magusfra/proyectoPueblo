Activities 2022-2023

5 jan, 2023 The word Melgar may be related to the word of Celtic origin ambelicas that derives from amelga, formed by the Celtic root el- (cir) and the prefix ambi- (environment) which means strip of land that the farmer points out in a haza ( portion of farmland) to spread the seed equally. The ending -AR would then be added, giving it the meaning of "land divided into "amelgas" or narrow farms".

9 to 10 jun 2023 The Astudillo Rock festival returns to full energy on June 10 and 11, 2022, as always, in the Palencia town of Astudillo, after two years of hiatus forced by the pandemic. The first day of the festival on Friday, June 10, will perform, starting at 10:00 p.m., Debakle, Oktopussy, The Fox 196 and Kodigo Punk. The next day it will be the turn of Los De Marras, El Último Ke Zierre, Dakidarría and Yo No Las Conozco starting at 9:30 p.m.
